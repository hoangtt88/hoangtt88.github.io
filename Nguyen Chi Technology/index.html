Solar Rooftop Solutions

Industrial halls and office building are characterized with their relatively high roof-to-floor ratio, which facilitates ready deployment of renewable energy generation, such as photovoltaic (PV) systems, on the rooftop. This particularly the case for industrial halls and office buildings, which are usually subject to high energy demand for space conditioning in order to remove the excess heat gain due to the many power-intensive processes. Otherwise, to promote deployment of renewable energy generation, feed-in tariff (FIT) higher than the electricity rate is available now in Vietnam to subsidize the capital investment. These rooftop solar plants have in most cases capacity of more than 1 MW, are installed on existent halls, buildings, require short installation time, high installation security. For the above mentioned rooftop PV application, we design, develop and outsource a Japanese company to produce the prefabricated PV mounting system made of light, non-rust, more 40 years long life Alloy-Aluminium. This mounting system reduces weight load on rooftop, installation-time, -costs, and increases thus installation security, high labor conditions.Our rooftop solar plant is equipped with the German first class Mono-crystalline Photovoltaic Module with 30 years warranty, Solar Inverter with 5 years warranty, and PV-Mounting System with 30 years warranty, for industrial application.

Biomass Solutions

With new development of steam boiler, steam turbine, investment of biomass power plant became lower 1.6 USD fer kW and generating electricity costs 0.15 USD fer kWh, and annular capacity factor is 85%, this means 7.440 hour fer year. All factors lead biomass power plant as the most profitable renewable energy. Biomass is carbon, sulfur neutral energy; emissions controls systems and electrostatic precipitator are used to reduce particulate matter and oxides of nitrogen emissions. Furthermore biomass, unlike sun and wind, is year round and constantly available as well as storage. Biomass briquetting reduces volume by up to 90%, makes easier to store, transport handle. Otherwise briquetting increases caloric value, density, fixed carbon, and requires smaller volume of burning furnace. Uniform physical dimensions & combustion characteristics, results in more efficient energy conversion. Briquettes burn smokeless, carbonless in a controlled manner, slow and efficiently because of drying, higher bulk density, & lower ash content. Briquetting burning furnace is cost efficient, environmental. Our partners as general contractors produce, plan and build biomass thermal power turnkey plant with state-of- the-art firing, customized steam turbine technology, flue gas cleaning systems of 100% ash recovery. Burning furnace, with turbulent and heated flow of combustion air, is designed for high burning temperature and for almost full burn of carbon in ash.
